What is a Triangle?

A triangle is a closed 3-sided polygon. Every triangle has three sides and three angles.

Types of Triangles:

On the basis of Sides:

Equilateral Triangle

  • An equilateral triangle is a triangle whose three sides all have the same length
  • All three sides are equal in length, and all three angles in the corners are 60º.

Isosceles Triangle

  • An equilateral triangle is a triangle whose three sides all have the same length
  • All three sides are equal in length, and all three angles in the corners are 60º.

Scalene Triangle

  • An equilateral triangle is a triangle whose three sides all have the same length
  • All three sides are equal in length, and all three angles in the corners are 60º.

Some Properties of Triangles:

  1. The sum of all angles of a traingle is equal to 180°. This is called the "Angle Sum Property".
  2. The sum of length of two sides of a triang;e is greater than lenght of the third side
  3. The side opposite the greater angle is the longest side of all the three sides of a triangle.
